About Me

So, who am I?
Well, my name is Wren, and I made this website. Yep. Everything I have entered into this site was input by yours truly.
I get asked a bunch of questions all the time; so as a lazy way of getting around that awkward conversation of getting to know someone, I have created a FAQs section, for all your curiosities.

FAQs

What are your hobbies?

W: I like to draw, read, write and craft, but most of all, be a mother.

Why miniatures?

W: Who doesn't love tiny, cute, adorable, little versions of life-size furniture and food? I first started getting into miniatures when I got my first 'polly pocket'. She was so small and tiny, I couldn't believe that I had something so detailed, yet so small. From that moment, I knew I'd become obsessed. And that was 19 years ago.

How difficult is it to create these miniatures?

W: I think it depends on what you aim to make and what size you're trying to achive. For me, I love the scale size 1:16; this means that whatever item you aim to make is 1/16th of it's original scale. So an example would be, a washing machine; it's original scale is Height: 85cm and Width: 60cm. In 16th scale, that would shrink down to around Height: 5.3cm and Width: 3.7cm.
Tiny, right?! How on earth would someone be able to detail such a complex model on such a small item? Patience and detemination...(and maybe a few blood, sweat, and teary moments).
